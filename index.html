Lago De Braise

Lago di Braies or Pragser Wildsee, Lake Braies is one of the most beautiful lakes in Italy. It is a bright blue mountain lake in the heart of the Dolomites.

Shirakawa-go

Shirakawa-gō, a small, traditional village in Japan, showcasing a building style known as gasshō-zukuri. It is one of UNESCO's World Heritage Sites.
